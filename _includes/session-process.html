What happens in a session

You bring whatever's going on. It doesn't have to be tidy.

We spend 50 minutes on it. You talk, I listen hard, and I reflect back what I'm actually hearing -- which is often not what you think you said. Then we work out what's real, what matters, and what you're going to do about it. This is not 50 minutes of talking about your feelings. You leave with something actionable.

Most people finish knowing what they're doing next. Some finish knowing they've been solving the wrong problem entirely. Both are useful.

Sessions are remote, by video or phone. If you'd rather be walking than staring at a screen, that works.
