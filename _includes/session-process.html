What happens in a remote 1:1 session, by video or phone

All sessions are remote. If you'd rather be away from your screen, on a walk or just outside, we can do that as a phone call instead. Your brain might appreciate it.

  1. 01

    We dive straight in

    No talking about the weather. No dancing round it. We get to the point.

  2. 02

    I get to the ugly little truth of it

    You tell me what is going on. We work out what matters, what is noise, where the pressure is coming from, and what has been quietly chewing through your energy.

  3. 03

    We figure out the next move

    We work out what to say, what to ask for, what to stop apologising for, and what move changes the shape of the problem.

  4. 04

    You stop carrying it alone

    You leave with a clearer head, a better plan, and far less chance of getting swallowed by panic, self-doubt, or someone else's agenda.
