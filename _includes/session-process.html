What happens in a session

You bring whatever's going on. It doesn't have to be tidy.

We'll spend 50 minutes getting to what's actually happening underneath it, not the version you've been telling everyone else.

You'll leave with a clear head and knowing exactly what you're doing next.

People are often surprised how much changes for them after one session.

Sessions are remote, by video or phone. If you'd rather be on a walk than staring at a screen, that works too.
