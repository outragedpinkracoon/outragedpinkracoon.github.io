Why work with me

I'm a practicing VP of Engineering, not a coach who left the industry a decade ago. I'm still in the weeds and at the exec table, often in the same day, so I know what these situations look like from every angle. I've coached people through missed promotions, hard pivots, burnout, bad bosses, and the quieter thing: wanting more and not quite knowing how to go get it.

I don't reduce being stuck, ambitious, or tired to "have you tried believing in yourself more?"

Whether something's on fire or you just know you're capable of more than this, what I bring is the same: focus, directness, good humour, and someone who has been in most of these situations and came out knowing what actually helps.

I'm autistic, which means I don't do performative nonsense. You get someone who will get to the actual thing faster than you expect, and be genuinely in your corner while we do it.
