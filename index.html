I'm Val — ILM certified coach, tech exec, and creator of Holy Sh*t I'm a Manager. I help tech people lead, survive, and grow without sanding off the parts of themselves that make them brilliant.

I offer practical training for new managers of all kinds, and 1:1 coaching for women, queer, non-binary, disabled, neurodivergent and otherwise under-represented people navigating confidence, power, burnout, visibility and difficult work situations in tech.

I spent nearly two decades navigating tech as a woman, before daring to come out as queer, non-binary, neurodivergent and disabled.

So I know what it costs to succeed inside systems that were never built with you in mind.

If you want corporate polish, I am probably not your person. If you want directness, kindness, lived experience, and someone who understands how broken the room can be before you even walk into it, welcome.

Racoons are animals that don't belong anywhere, but thrive anyway!
