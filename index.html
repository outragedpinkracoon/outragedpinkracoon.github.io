I'm Val — ILM certified coach, tech exec, and creator of Holy Shit I'm a Manager. I help under-represented people in tech lead, survive, grow and stay themselves inside systems that were never built with them in mind.

The main thing I offer is remote 1:1 coaching for women, queer, non-binary, disabled, neurodivergent and otherwise under-represented tech people dealing with confidence, power, burnout, visibility, management and difficult work situations.

I spent nearly two decades surviving tech as a woman, before daring to come out as queer, non-binary, neurodivergent and disabled.

So I know what it costs to succeed inside systems that were never built with you in mind.

If you want corporate polish, I am probably not your person. If you want directness, kindness, lived experience, and someone who understands how broken the room can be before you even walk into it, welcome.

Racoons are animals that don't belong anywhere, but thrive anyway!

Remote 1:1 coaching

For capable people who are exhausted from being treated like the problem.

Remote, private coaching for under-represented folks in tech who are tired of shrinking, masking, over-performing, burning out, or trying to make sense of broken systems alone.

We might work on confidence after a bad manager, visibility without self-abandonment, asking for accommodations, deciding whether to stay or leave, preparing for promotion, handling a difficult stakeholder, or learning to lead without copying the patterns that harmed you.
