Real Managers Saying Real Things!
Often as managers when we deal with new situations we come to them having some existing notions in our head that may not reflect reality, so instead of coming to a situation with assumption and an imagined picture come from a place of curiosity and ask questions first. Having tried this technique myself over the last few months (credit to Valerie Dryden for opening my eyes to the value of it) I have to say that the conversations I had with people I support and meetings that I attended had noticeably better outcomes all while reducing my stress levels.
