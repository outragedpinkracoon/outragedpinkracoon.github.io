<p class="page-intro">Outraged Racoon Coaching&reg; is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and what we do with it.</p>

<h2>Who we are</h2>

<p>Outraged Racoon Coaching&reg; is a UK-based sole trader coaching and consultancy business, governed by Scottish law.</p>

<p>Registered with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO).<br>
Contact: <a href="mailto:outragedracooncoaching@gmail.com">outragedracooncoaching@gmail.com</a></p>

<h2>What we collect</h2>

<h3>Website visits</h3>

<p>We use <a href="https://www.simpleanalytics.com" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Simple Analytics<span class="sr-only"> (opens in new tab)</span></a> for website analytics. It does not use cookies and does not collect or store personal data. No tracking, no fingerprinting, no consent banner needed.</p>

<h3>Enquiries</h3>

<p>If you contact us by email, we collect your name, email address, and anything you choose to share. We use this to respond to you and, if relevant, to follow up about services.</p>

<h3>Coaching clients</h3>

<p>If you work with us directly, we may hold:</p>

<ul>
  <li>Contact details</li>
  <li>Invoice and payment records</li>
  <li>Session notes where relevant to your coaching work</li>
</ul>

<p>We keep this minimal. You can ask what we hold at any time.</p>

<h2>How we use your data</h2>

<p>We use your data to deliver coaching and consultancy services, respond to enquiries, manage invoicing, and send administrative information. We do not send marketing emails unless you have explicitly opted in, and you can unsubscribe at any time.</p>

<h2>Legal basis (UK GDPR)</h2>

<ul>
  <li><strong>Contract</strong> — to deliver services you have requested</li>
  <li><strong>Legitimate interests</strong> — to run and improve the business</li>
  <li><strong>Consent</strong> — for any marketing communications</li>
</ul>

<h2>Data sharing</h2>

<p>We do not sell your data. We may use trusted service providers (email, payment processing) who handle data on our behalf under appropriate data protection agreements. Some providers may process data outside the UK; where they do, appropriate safeguards apply under UK GDPR.</p>

<h2>How long we keep it</h2>

<p>Enquiry data is retained for a reasonable follow-up period. Client session notes and records are held for up to six months after the coaching relationship ends, in line with our <a href="/coaching-disclaimer-and-terms/">terms and conditions</a>. Invoice and payment records are kept for six years as required by HMRC. You can request deletion of other data at any time.</p>

<h2>Your rights</h2>

<p>Under UK GDPR you have the right to access, correct, or delete your data; to restrict or object to processing; and to data portability. You also have the right to complain to the <a href="https://ico.org.uk" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Information Commissioner's Office<span class="sr-only"> (opens in new tab)</span></a>.</p>

<p>To exercise any of these rights, contact <a href="mailto:outragedracooncoaching@gmail.com">outragedracooncoaching@gmail.com</a>.</p>

<h2>Security</h2>

<p>We take reasonable steps to protect your data. No system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security, but we treat your information with care.</p>

<h2>Changes to this policy</h2>

<p>We may update this policy from time to time. The current version is always at this URL. Continued use of our services after a change means you accept the updated policy.</p>
