<h2>This is the bit nobody hands you on day one</h2>

<p>You got the promotion because you were good at the job. Now the job is making other people good at the job, and those are completely different jobs. Nobody told you that either.</p>

<p>So here's what you'll actually be able to do by the end:</p>

<ul class="course-wins">
  <li>Run one-to-ones that don't suck the life out of you or them.</li>
  <li>Give feedback that lands, without anyone crying. Including you.</li>
  <li>Manage your boss as skillfully as you manage your team.</li>
  <li>Have the hard conversation you've been avoiding, and survive it.</li>
  <li>Keep your shit together when everything is on fire.</li>
  <li>Know what good looks like, so you stop guessing.</li>
</ul>
