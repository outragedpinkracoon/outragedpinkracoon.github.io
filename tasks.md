**Outraged Racoon Coaching — Website Overhaul Brief**

**Context**

Val is a Fractional CTO and Executive Coach. The website was built when the business was primarily focused on under-represented folks in tech. The business has grown significantly and the site hasn't kept up. Val now serves three audiences: founders/CTOs, engineering managers, and folks in tech (including neurodivergent, queer, disabled and under-represented people). She also works as a Fractional CTO through Rational Partners.

The LinkedIn bio and banner have been overhauled today and the website needs to match that energy. The new positioning is:

**No-bullsh*t coaching for the messy, human, hard parts of working in tech.**

---

**Who Val Is Now**

- Nearly two decades in tech — engineer, manager, VP, Acting CTO
- 5+ multi-million dollar transformations in companies up to $300M ARR
- Cut cycle times by 60%, incidents by 50%, defects by 40% across multiple orgs
- Saved £1m+ annually through delivery transformation and AI adoption, multiple times
- Cloud migrations with zero downtime, ISO audits delivered
- Cultures moved from fear to empowerment, diversity increased by 55-60%
- Coached 300+ people including 100+ founders, ILM Level 5 certified
- Co-created Scotland's first coding academy
- Created Holy Sh*t I'm a Manager™ — 100+ graduates, 200+ member community
- Neurodivergent, queer, non-binary, disabled — not an ally looking in, someone standing in the same weather

**Pages Needing Work**

**1. Who Is Val — full rewrite**

This is the priority. Current page leads with identity before authority. Needs to flip.

Target structure:
- Open with authority — the receipts, the track record, the weight of it
- Bring in coaching philosophy
- Identity and lived experience as the differentiator, not the headline
- Keep "I coach the people I used to be" — it's the best line on the site
- Keep the human bit at the bottom (art, Stakeholders, TTRPG etc)
- Add the line about never having had a manager who looked like her — it's the why behind everything
- Tone: same as the rest of the site — direct, warm, no corporate theatre

Key line to keep: *"I coach the people I used to be — including the neurodivergent, disabled and queer version of me."*

**2. Coaching landing page — light refresh**

The three buckets (EM, founders/CTOs, folks in tech) are fine and staying. The intro copy needs to better reflect the new positioning — messy, human, hard parts — rather than just listing the three audiences.

**3. Founders/CTOs page — add retainer option**

Currently has single session (£400), 3-session bundle (£1,000), and 6-session programme (£2,000). Needs a fourth option below the 6-session programme for ongoing retainer support — for founders who want Val in their corner long-term beyond the programme. Pricing TBD but should sit above £2,000/month territory. Copy should make clear this is for the long game, not a defined programme.

**4. All coaching pages — minor**

The header strip currently says: *ILM Certified Coach | Tech Exec | Neurodivergent | Queer | Non-binary | A Little Feral*

Consider updating to better reflect the dual positioning (Fractional CTO + Coach) without losing the personality.

**5. All coaching pages — credentials before empathy**

The LinkedIn bio energy needs to carry into the site. Each coaching page currently leads with empathy (audience pain, relatable mess) before establishing authority. The rewrite principle from Who Is Val applies here in lighter form: bring a credentials moment — weight, receipts, track record — earlier in the page, before the empathy copy. Trust first, warmth second. Same voice, different sequence.

Someone landing on any page — whether a founder, a CTO via Rational Partners, or a burned out neurodivergent engineer — should immediately think "this person is the real deal and gets it." Currently the site gets there eventually. It needs to get there faster.
